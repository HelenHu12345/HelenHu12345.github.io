Types of Motivation

Learn and explore the different ways you can become motivated!

Intrinsic Motivation

When individuals are intrinsically motivated, they do activities for their own sake and out of interest in the activity.

Extrnsic Motivation

When extrinsically motivated, individuals do activities for instrumental or other reasons, such as receiving a reward

Mastery goal

Adopting a mastery goal orientation means that the person is focused on improving their skills, mastering material, and learning new things.

Performance goal

Adopting a performance or ego orientation means that the person focuses on maximizing favorable evaluations of their competence and minimizing negative evaluations of competence.

Self-efficacy

Self-efficacy refers to one's sense of confidence or perceived competence in successding in a task.

Flow
